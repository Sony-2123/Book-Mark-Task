Whodunit

            The story begins with the history of the Moonstone, a large, valuable yellow diamond stolen from a Hindu temple in India. The diamond is said to be cursed and protected by Indian priests who are sworn to recover it.             As the months pass, Franklin is tormented by the unsolved mystery. Determined to clear his name, he resumes the investigation with the help of Ezra Jennings, an assistant to Dr. Candy, a physician who had attended the birthday party.

            Epilogue: Justice and Resolution
            With the case solved, Franklin and Rachel reconcile and marry. The Moonstone is restored to its rightful place in the temple in India, ending the curse that haunted it.
